Perfect Movies According To Rafael

My absolute favorites

1) Kill Bill (both parts)

x

Caution, spoilers!!

The movie is about a girl who had previously been an assasin.
She was working for a guy named Bill who had a whole band of assasins.
Then the girl decided to quit and have a quiet and a peaceful life somewhere in the woods.
So then she gets shot by Bill and the other assasins while pregnant at her own wedding
Somehow she stays alive and plans a big and bloody revenge to all of them

Rating: A solid 9/10.

2) The Godfather (trilogy)

First two parts are the greatest mafia movies ever, but the third part wasn't on the same level.
Absolute masterpiece with Al Pacino playing the goat himself Michael Corleone.

Rating: First two parts are 9.5/10 but overall the trilogy itself is a 8.5/10
